## AlphaPath: Toward a Search-Based Architecture for Professional Knowledge Navigation

> Paz, H. R. (2026). *AlphaPath. Toward a Search-Based Architecture for Professional
> Knowledge Navigation (v1.0.0).* Zenodo.
> https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.19552912

**Abstract (excerpt):**
Introduces the AlphaPath framework for curriculum architecture analysis and search-based
design exploration. Develops the Professional Competence Unit representation model, the
staged evaluation stack, and the claim discipline principle.

## Toward a New Curricular Architecture for Civil Engineering in the Age of Artificial Intelligence

> Paz, H. R. (2026). *Toward a New Curricular Architecture for Civil Engineering in the
> Age of Artificial Intelligence.* Zenodo.
> https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.19591967

**Abstract (excerpt):**
Applies the AlphaPath framework to Civil Engineering education. Develops a taxonomy of
professional learning in the AI era. Introduces AI-era PCU extensions to the Civil
Engineering domain ontology. Compares conservative, hybrid, and search-based curricular
architectures under the AlphaPath evaluation stack.
