# Data Availability

This public repository does not include raw institutional data, student-level records,
or confidential datasets.

The AlphaPath research programme uses data and derived artefacts that are subject to
privacy, institutional confidentiality, and research governance constraints.

---

## Included in This Repository

- synthetic examples (illustrative only, not derived from institutional data);
- public summary tables;
- selected non-sensitive figures;
- public documentation.

---

## Excluded

- raw institutional data;
- processed institutional datasets;
- student-level records;
- internal experiment bundles;
- confidential institutional records;
- private analysis materials;
- unpublished manuscript drafts.

---

## Synthetic Data

Any data included under [examples/](examples/) is synthetic and intended only to
illustrate the general logic of curriculum graph representation or simplified evaluation.
It was not derived from real student records, institutional systems, or confidential
administrative data.

---

Requests for access to restricted research materials may be directed to the author
