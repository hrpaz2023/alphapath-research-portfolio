# AlphaPath Research Portfolio

**AlphaPath** is a research-grade framework for curriculum architecture analysis,
search-based design exploration, and evaluation of professional learning trajectories.

This public repository provides a curated portfolio of the AlphaPath research programme,
including conceptual documentation, synthetic examples, selected non-sensitive outputs,
manuscript mappings, and reproducibility notes.

The full research codebase, institutional datasets, internal experimental pipelines,
and unpublished working materials are not publicly released.

---

## What AlphaPath Does

AlphaPath treats curriculum not merely as a sequence of subjects, but as an architectural
object that can be represented, analysed, searched, evaluated, and compared.

It supports:

- curriculum architecture analysis;
- graph-based representation of progression structures;
- search-based exploration of candidate curricular designs;
- institutional translation and packaging-aware evaluation;
- simulation- and uncertainty-aware comparison;
- manuscript-level traceability between experiments, metrics, figures, and claims.
